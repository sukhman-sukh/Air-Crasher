Air-Crasher

Instructions

You have 3 balls , 2 are Enemy Balls Coloured With :- Green and Red

And You Have A Player Ball Coloured With Yellow




Your Task Is To Confine Those EnemyBalls In Between Tiles In Small Area, To Win The Game

EnemyBalls Will Keep On Destroying The Tiles And You Have To Beat Them Before Your Death !!



Everytime You Move Your Player Ball From One Tile To Other , It Will Create A Tile Wall At Path Covered

If You Move Your Player Ball In Straight Line Forming A Line Of Tiles Between Top-Bottom Or Left-Right
It Will Fill One Side Of It ( Other Than The One Containing Enemy Balls ) Completely With Tiles

If You Face Collision with the Enemy Balls , You Lose The Game

If You Are Not At Any Tile And The White Dots Showing Your Path Comes In Contact With Enemy Balls You Lose
